1.0 Purpose 目的:

Through the Layered Process Audit to manufacturing related processes to verify the process meets the defined requirement, and issue corrective activities were implemented to make sure the process is under control.

通过对制造相关过程的分层审核，确认过程控制符合规定的要求，问题改进措施在过程中得到落实，确保过程受控。


2.0 Scope 范围:

LPA is applicable to Incoming Quality Inspection, in-plant logistics management, Block and Head machining, ATPU, engine rework, finished engine warehousing storage and dispatch,

LPA 适用于BFCEC进料检验、厂内物料管理、缸体缸盖加工过程、物料配送、ATPU过程、发动机返工过程以及成品发动机的存放和发运过程的分层审核的计划和实施

%1.0 Responsibilities 职责

4.1 Quality Department (QUA) 质量部

QUA LPA Coordinator is responsible for LPA Planning, organizing and implementing, updating the issue list and the Monthly LPA checklist, verifying and confirming the status of the audit findings.

质量部LPA协调员负责过程分层审核的策划、组织与实施，制定并更新LPA审核检查表和月度LPA审核清单，验证和确认审核问题状态。


4.2 Manufacturing Department(MFG) 制造部

MFG LPA Coordinator is responsible for MFG daily LPA organizing and facilitating, Monthly summarizing and submitting LPA record and findings to QUA, organizing and facilitating the audit issue solving, and escalating to QUA when issue is unable to solve.

MFG LPA协调员负责推动制造部日常LPA审核、分月总结，提交审核记录及审核发现的问题给质量部，组织并推进本部门审核问题的解决，无法解决的问题及时升级报质量部。


4.3 Material Department (MAT) 材料部


MAT LPA Coordinator is responsible for MAT daily LPA organizing and facilitating, Monthly summarizing and submitting LPA record and findings to QUA, organizing and facilitating the audit issue solving, and escalating to QUA when issue is unable to solve.

MAT LPA协调员推动物料部日常LPA审核、分月总结，提交审核记录及审核发现的问题给质量部，组织并推进本部门审核问题的解决，无法解决的问题升级及时报质量部。


4.4 Other Departments问题的责任部门

Department that has issue found through the audit shall take corrective actions or carry out the improvement, and deliver corrective reports as required.

审核发现问题的责任部门负责实施问题的纠正和改进，并按照要求提供问题改进报告。


%1.0 Definitions定义:

5.1 LPA：Layered Process Audit. It is standardized evaluation process based on the audit and evaluation by every levels of people who will participate in evaluating and reviewing the production process by the predefined frequency to assure the manufacturing process is under control and strengthen the concept of Lean Production.

分层审核：由各级人员按照预先计划的频次定期参与评审并回顾整改为基础的标准化的评审过程，以确保制造过程受控并加强精益制造 理念。


5.2 LPA Auditors Competency LPA审核员能力要求

Understanding of BFCEC engines structure and manufacture process.

了解福田康明斯发动机结构和制造过程

Understanding of applicable customer-specific requirements.

了解适用的顾客特定要求。

Understanding requirements related to the scope of the audit.

了解与审核范围有关的要求。

Understand of how to plan，conduct，report，and close out audit findings

了解如何计划审核，实施审核，报告审核以及关闭审核发现。

LPA Auditors shall be yearly internal certificated

LPA审核员需要做年度内部资格认证。
